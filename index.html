Projects

මනුදම් වියමන

In the heart of communities where need often goes unnoticed, the Rotaract food donation campaign, Manudam Wiyamana, emerges as a beacon of compassion. This initiative, driven by the collective spirit of Rotaract members, seeks to address the pressing issue of hunger and food insecurity.

Children’s Day Celebrations

In celebration of Children's Day, we had the privilege to visit Maw Piya Sewana Children's Home for a day filled with meaningful activities. The genuine joy, laughter, and smiles of the children left a lasting impression, serving as a profound reminder of the significance of nurturing and celebrating the spirit of childhood.

Zoo Waste

The Dehiwala Zoological Gardens is a treasured home to a diverse range of endangered and endemic animals. Through this initiative, we aimed to minimize waste generation and promote responsible waste disposal practices within the zoo premises, fostering a harmonious coexistence between wildlife and preservation efforts.
